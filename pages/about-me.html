Info

I am PhD student in the RoboticsLab at Carlos III University of Madrid.

My research interests interests: 1. high-level planning algorithms: robot formations, previous experience... 2. low level planning algorithms: improving computational time. 3. 3D mapping (as a hobby).

Side interests include open-source robotics, mainly OMPL, and GPS-less global positioning systems, SGPS.

My hobbies are mountain biking, hiking and travelling. In fact, I have lived in: 1. Madrid, Spain. 2. Oslo, Norway. 3. Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. 4. Stuttgart, Germany. 6. Athens, Greece. 5. San Francisco Bay Area, CA, USA.
